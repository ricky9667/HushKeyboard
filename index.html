Introduction

Why HUSH Keyboard?

Typing Rubik's Cube notations on a mobile keyboard kind of a frustration, switching between notations, numbers, and symbols makes it extremely annoying.
HUSH Keyboard provides a specialized Rubik's Cube keyboard that is functional, minimal, and customizable.

Features

HINTS

Tooltip hints

Preview your notation just like Google's Gboard.

CONTROLS

State control

Just one click to type 180 degree, counter-clockwise, and even wide notations.

SWIPE

Gesture input

Use swipe gestures to type faster than Feliks Zemdegs.
