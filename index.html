Typing notations is much easier.

Typing Rubik's cube notations on a mobile keyboard is just a torture, it is very annoying to switch between letters, numbers, and symbols. HUSH Keyboard puts the notation you need in one minimal, customizable keyboard.

Fewer switches. Faster notation.

Preview each move before it lands, change notation state with one tap, or swipe through common variations. Makes you type faster than Feliks Zemdegs.

Choose your own keyboard style.

HUSH Keyboard follows your system light/dark theme. You can also set your keyboard theme in app settings.
